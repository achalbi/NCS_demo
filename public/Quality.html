QUALITY

NCS is committed to being very aggressive in our approach towards quality and customer service, primarily since the organizational objective is to be ranked among the "best" in our business. Quality is not just another goal, it is our basic strategy for survival and future growth.

Total Quality Management to NCS is fully satisfying our customers requirements through a process of continuous improvement. Total Quality Management is not a short term program. It's a long term commitment aimed at continuously improving the way we work, managing our business processes, and meeting customer expectations.

Quality Standards Adaptation
ISO 9001:2008 has been adapted to ensure zero-defects. High quality levels are maintained by adhering to the following practices:

  • Standard Operating Procedures
  • Checklists - Project and Customer based
  • Corrective Action
  • Non Conformance Reports & 8D Reports
  • Customized training Manuals and Plans
  • Use of statistical tools for categorizations, mapping and individual skill sets
  • Team focused on enhancing competencies & continuous improvement
